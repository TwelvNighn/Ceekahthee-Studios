What is Spam and Milk 2: Milkening?


Spam and Milk 2: Milkening is the sequel to the wad 'Spam and Milk' made by CBM
You play as 'Milkinator', who is on a date with the famous 'Milkman'. Everything goes well, until the time of the bill comes.
Milkman tricks her and tries to run away, but Milkinator tries to stop her...
Join Milkinator in her journey as she goes through bars, factories, trains, and towns.
